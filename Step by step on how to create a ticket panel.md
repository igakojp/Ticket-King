Before you do this one, if you don't have Ticket King in your server, check this one out first.  
[How to add Ticket King into your server](https://github.com/igakojp/Ticket-King/blob/main/How%20to%20add%20Ticket%20King%20into%20your%20server.md)

Now you have the bot in your server, let's start.

First, go to your server you want to make the panel on. Make sure that the bot is in the server.

Use the command `/panel` to create the panel on the channel you want the panel to be on.

The basic panel will be sent by the bot. If it shows **thinking...** or **Needs permission**, then please contact the staff in this support server and we will help you.  
(When you create a ticket, please first provide your server ID.)

[Support server](https://ticketking.xyz/discord)

If it makes the panel correctly, go to the [dashboard](https://ticketking.xyz/dashboard).

Click on **Manage Server** for the server you added the panel to.

You are done!
